BIOGRAPHY

Chris 'DutchMash' Maas

Freelance photographer, videographer and motion artist based in the Netherlands.

My love for videography and photography began at a young age. When I was little, I always stole a handycam from my parents to create videos with friends and I would watch tutorials about software my PC couldn't even run back in the time. In 2014 I started a YouTube channel around a videogame - League of Legends. The channel managed to reach 180k subscribers and over 80 million views.

I kept improving myself and since 2016 I've been working with Fnatic, one of the leaders in the esports world.

Excelling at work within the esports industry:

  • 6+ years experience growing YouTube channels
  • 3+ years experience in photography, videography, motion and content strategy for Fnatic
